Pronunciation Tip

For the 'th' sounds, place the tip of your tongue between your teeth and blow air out. For /Œ∏/ (as in "think"), don't use your voice. For /√∞/ (as in "the"), use your voice.

Pronunciation Tip

Listen to native speakers and try to mimic not just the sounds but also the rhythm and melody of their speech. Watching English movies or TV shows with subtitles can help you connect the written words with their natural pronunciation.
